About

The knowledge registration system is a system that allows programmers to indicate their level of proficiency in various areas, with the possibility of sorting and filtering. Used for further selection of specialists by the manager.

Description of levels:

 - choose the «Beginner» if you have some theoretical knowledge.

 - choose «Intermediate» if you have good theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

 - choose «Advanced» if you have excellent theoretical knowledge, have practical skills and work experience more than 1 year.

 - choose «Expert» if you have advanced theoretical knowledge, have excellent practical skills, work experience is more than 3 years and completed at least 2 serious projects with a key requirement for this skill.

- choose «Godlike» if you excellently possess theoretical and practical skills, have more than 10 years of experience, completed more than 10 serious projects with a key requirement for this skill, have experience in developing a world-famous project.
